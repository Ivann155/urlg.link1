Frequently Asked Questions

How does URL shortening work?

URL shortening works by taking a long URL and creating a much shorter, unique code that redirects to the original link. When someone visits the short URL, our system automatically redirects them to the original destination. It's like creating a shortcut that's easier to share and remember.

Are my shortened links permanent?

Yes! All shortened links created through urlG.link are permanent and will continue to work indefinitely. Once you create a short link, it will always redirect to your original URL unless you specifically delete it.

Can I customize my short links?

Currently, our system generates pronounceable, memorable codes automatically. We're working on custom alias features that will allow you to create your own custom short links in the future.

Is there a limit to how many links I can shorten?

No limits! You can shorten as many URLs as you want. Our service is designed to handle unlimited link shortening for all users, completely free of charge.

How secure are my shortened links?

Your shortened links are completely secure. We don't store any personal information, and the short codes are randomly generated, making them impossible to guess. Your original URLs remain private and secure.
